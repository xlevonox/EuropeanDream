Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 8, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game European Dream developed by Levonox.

Information We Collect

European Dream does not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

However, the app uses third-party services provided by Google Firebase to improve stability and understand basic usage trends:

  • Firebase Analytics – collects anonymous data such as screen views, session duration, and device type to understand how players interact with the game.
  • Firebase Crashlytics – collects crash reports and basic diagnostic data (such as device model, operating system version, and error logs) to help fix technical issues.

This information is processed by Google according to their Privacy Policy and is not used to personally identify you.

Data Storage

All gameplay progress and local settings are stored on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Other Features

The game includes an optional “Rate this app” button that opens the Google Play Store page for feedback. No information is collected during this action.

Your Consent

By using European Dream, you consent to the collection and processing of anonymous diagnostic and analytics data as described above.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at: xlevonox@gmail.com.

Thank you for playing European Dream.
